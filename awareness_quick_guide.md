# Cybersecurity Quick Guide

## 1. Passwords
- Use strong, unique passwords for every account.
- Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) wherever possible.

## 2. Phishing
- Do not click on links or attachments from unknown senders.
- Verify unexpected requests via phone or alternate channels.

## 3. Devices
- Keep software and operating systems updated.
- Lock your devices when unattended.

## 4. Data Handling
- Avoid storing sensitive data on local devices unnecessarily.
- Encrypt data in transit and at rest where applicable.

## 5. Reporting
- Immediately report any suspicious emails, activity, or incidents to your IT/security team.
